TREATY ONE MAP

Treaty 1 was signed in 1871. It was one of the first treaties between Canada and First Nations that lived around the area of what we now call Winnipeg, Manitoba. First Nations such as: Anishinabe, Swampy Cree Nations, etc. wanted to create peacefull relationships with settlers and maintain their traditional land. As they saw changes that came with newcommers, they wanted security to transition to the new way of life, so they signed traties with Canada.
