TREATY ONE

Treaty 1 was signed in 1871. It was one of the first treaties between Canada and First Nations that lived around the area of what we now call Winnipeg, Manitoba. First Nations such as: Anishinabe, Swampy Cree Nations, etc. wanted to creat peacefull relationships with settlers and maintain their traditional land. As they saw changes that came with newcommers, they wanted security to transition to the new way of life, so they signed traties with Canada.

FIRST NATIONS

First Nations are groups of indigenous people, original to this land for thousands of years. They are many diffrent Nations with their own traditions, languages and culture. Indigenous people have deep connections to the land, animals, and nature, that are a part of their art, music and stories passed down for generations.
